Feature of the month

Days Gone Pie is nothing without the community of bakers that support the site. We rely on you as members to help fill our recipe books with amazing things that put smiles on faces. We want everyone who visits us here to feel right at home, so every month we put one of our amazing members in the spotlight and let them tell you a little bit about themselves and select a special recipe that they think you will really love.

The Saxon Forager

{% for image in feature %} {% endfor %}

Craig Brooks lives in Wiltshire (UK). He is better known to the online community as 'The Saxon Forager', a historical re-enactor and one man baking/brewing sensation. In his new book, 'Eat Like a Viking' Vol.2, Craig has continued his work, attempting recreate some of the food and drink that would have been eaten by our Anglo Saxon fore-fathers.

Osterhlafas (Oyster loaf) is one of the recipes in 'Eat Like a Viking' Vol.2, and Craig has been kind enough to share it with us. We asked craig why he'd decided to share this particular recipe with us. "I'd considered making this recipe for some time, and working on content for my next book gave me the perfect opportunity to give it a go. I like it as it's a little on the unusual side and I was able to create something that I hadn't seen before."
